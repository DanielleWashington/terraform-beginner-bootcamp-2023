What could be better than the smooth, elegant sounds of jazz blended with the quick, lively sounds of samba?

Bossa Nova!

Bossa Nova, which literally translates to "new wave" is the love-child of the jazz and samba genres. Born in the Copacabana section of Rio, Bossa Nova has enchanted the world with its infectious, easy-going sounds. Many are enamored with classics like "The Girl from Ipanema," "Corcovado," and even "Desafinado" and if you haven't yet heard them, do yourself that favor!
